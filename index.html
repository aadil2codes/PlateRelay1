Our Mission

PlateRelay was built to solve a simple but powerful problem: while millions of people struggle with hunger, tons of edible food are wasted every day.

Our mission is to connect restaurants, college messes, hotels, and event organizers with nearby NGOs so surplus food can reach people who need it instead of being thrown away.

By using real-time technology and location-based coordination, PlateRelay transforms food waste into community support.

Reducing Food Waste

PlateRelay helps reduce food waste by enabling providers to quickly post surplus food that would otherwise be discarded.

Real-Time Coordination

NGOs receive instant updates when surplus food is posted and can accept pickups immediately.

Community Impact

Every successful pickup helps feed communities and reduces the environmental impact of food waste.

Platform Features

Real-Time Tracking

Food listings appear instantly on the platform. Once an NGO accepts a pickup, the listing is automatically updated to prevent duplicate claims.

Simple Food Posting

Food providers can quickly post surplus food by entering details like quantity, location, and pickup time.

Verified Participants

The platform uses authentication and role management to ensure only registered NGOs and food providers can participate.

Location-Based Discovery

Using map integration, NGOs can easily locate nearby food providers and plan pickups efficiently.
